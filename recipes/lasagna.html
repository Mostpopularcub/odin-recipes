Lasagna

Description

A delicious, Italian classic that has been enjoyed at family gatherings throughout the centuries

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instrutions.
  5. Cover the foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
