Lasagna

Description

A sweet and spicy dish that astonishes and brings comfort at the same time.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Carrots
  • Ginger Root
  • Chicken Breast Halves
  • Water
  • Peanuts
  • Orange Juice

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil; cook and stir carrots and ginger.
  2. Cook and stir chicken in hot olive oil until no pink in center.
  3. Add carrot mixture, water, peanuts, orange juice, soy sauce, brown sugar, ketchup, and red pepper flakes to chicken; stir to combine.
